On the Street in Brooklyn

Call I'm a fashion designer and people think, what do I know? I have an obsession with details and pattern. I've always thought of the T-shirt as the Alpha and Omega of the fashion alphabet. I never like to think that I design for a particular person. I design for the woman I wanted to be, the woman I used to be, and - to some degree - the woman I'm still a little piece of. There has to be a balance between your mental satisfaction and the financial needs of your company.
